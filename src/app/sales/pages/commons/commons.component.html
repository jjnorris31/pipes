Date pipe

Date pipe to show formatted dates

  • {{today | date:'shortTime'}}
    today | date:'shortTime'
  • {{today | date:'medium'}}
    today | date:'medium'
  • {{today | date:'mm:ss'}}
    today | date:'mm:ss'
  • {{today | date:'full'}}
    today | date:'full'
  • {{today | date:'long':'GMT-5'}}
    today | date:'long':'GMT-5'
  • {{today | date:'long':'GMT-5':'en'}}
    today | date:'medium':'en'
  • {{today | date:'long':'GMT-5':'fr'}}
    today | date:'medium':'fr'
